About

Are you struggling with updating your resume? Need to create a new resume and don’t know where to start? Perhaps you are a career-changer and aren’t sure how to make your past work experience seem relevant to your new career choice. William T. Wisniewski is here to help. William has over 20 years of experience working and teaching in Human Resources. He knows what employers look for in potential job candidates. He wants to help people like you stand out and get their foot in the door.

A lot has changed over the past decade in regards to finding a new job. Applicant Tracking Systems or ATS, have made getting your resume in front of human eyes increasingly more difficult. Many qualified applicants are rejected by these systems because their resume doesn’t contain the right keywords required by the ATS. William T. Wisniewski can show you how to use the right keywords in your resume to get past the ATS and in front of the hiring manager.

Got an interview? William T. Wisniewski can help you answer interview questions with confidence. We’ll go over the most common interview questions and how to answer them. Learn what to say and what not to say. Present yourself in your best possible light and stand out among all the other candidates. Your next job is out there. Let William T. Wisniewski help bring you together.
